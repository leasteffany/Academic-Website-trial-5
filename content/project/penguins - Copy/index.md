### <dfn title="I have yet to perfect this">Chiffon Cake</dfn>

#### Dry Ingredients

>1/2 cup APF

>1/4 cup sugar

>1/2 tsp baking powder

>1/8 tsp salt

>2 tbsp. powdered milk

#### Wet Ingredients

> 2 egg yolks

> 1/8 cup water

> 1/8 cup oil

> 1/8 tsp vanilla

#### Meringue

> 2 egg whites

> 1/4 cup sugar

> 1/4 tsp vinegar

> ##### Instructions:
>
> 1. Beat egg whites until milky. Add vinegar slowly until soft peaks. Add in sugar slowly until stiff peaks.
>
> 2. Mix the dry ingredients. In a separate bowl, mix wet ingredients
>
> 3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients
>
> 4. Fold the merigue gently
>
> 5. Steam for 25 mins. in medium heat


### Banana Cake

#### Dry Ingredients

> 1 cup APF

> 3/4 tbsp baking powder

> 1/4 tbsp baking soda

> 1/4 tbsp cinnamon powder (optional)


#### Wet Ingredients

> 1/2 cup mashed banana 

> 1 egg

>1/2 cup brown sugar

>1/4 cup vegetable oil 

>1/2 cup milk

>1/2 tsp vanilla

>1 tsp vanilla

>pinch of salt

> ##### Instructions:
>
> 1. Combine wet ingredients
>
> 2. In a separate bowl, sift the dry ingredients
>
> 3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients
>
> 4. Steam for 25 mins. in medium heat

### Puto

#### Dry Ingredients

> 2 cups APF

> 1 cup powdered milk

> 2 tbsp baking powder



#### Wet Ingredients

> 1 egg white

> 1 cup sugar

> 2 tbsp. oil

> 2 cups water

> 1 tsp. vanilla



> ##### Instructions:
>
> 1. Combine wet ingredients
>
> 2. In a separate bowl, sift the dry ingredients
>
> 3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients
>
> 4. Steam for 10-12 mins. in medium heat

### Chocolate Birthday cake

#### Dry Ingredients

> 1 ¾ cup flour

> ¾ cup cocoa

> ½ tsp baking soda

> 1 tsp baking powder



#### Wet Ingredients

> 1 cup buttermilk (1 cup milk + 1 tbsp. sugar, rest for 15 mins)

> 1 ¾ cup sugar

> 2 eggs

> ½ cup vegetable oil

> 1 tsp vanilla

> 1 tsp salt

> 1 cup water with 1 tsp coffee


> ##### Instructions:
>
> 1. Mix and sift dry ingredients
>
> 2. Combine sugar, egg, oil, vanilla, salt MIX
>
> 3. Add buttermilk
>
> 4. Add ½ of the dry ingredients to wet, add half of the coffee mixture
>
> 5.Add remaining half of dry ingredients and remaining coffee
> 
>steam for 25 mins (make 4 llanera)

### Chocolate Frosting (ala red ribbon)

#### Dry Ingredients

> 1 cup sugar

> ½ cup cocoa

> 1 can evap (410 mL milk)

> 5 tbsp APF

> ¼ cup water

> ¼ cup butter


> ##### Instructions:
>
> 1. Combine ingredients except butter
>
> 2. Cook in medium heat while stirring constantly
>
> 3. When it's thick, add butter

### Ube Cake

#### Dry Ingredients

> 1 and 1/4 + 1/2 cup APF


> 1 tbsp baking powder



#### Wet Ingredients

> 1/2 cup evap and 1/2 cup water OR 1/2 cup milk powder and 1 cup water

> 1 cup sugar 

> 1/4 cup oil

> 2 cups water

> 1 egg

> 1 tsp salt and vanilla

> ube color and flavor



> ##### Instructions:
>
> 1. Combine wet ingredients
>
> 2. In a separate bowl, sift the dry ingredients
>
> 3. Combine dry to wet ingredients
>
